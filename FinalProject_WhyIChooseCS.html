Before Choosing CS

Before high school, I wanted to be an accountant. This suggested to me a stable career with a steady income to help support my family. However, after taking an accounting course in my sophomore year, I realized I was not interested in the subject at all.

What Changed My Mind

Later in my junior year, I took a computer science course just for fun. Surprisingly, I found myself engaged in class and excited about the projects. I also enjoyed the problem-solving process. I felt proud when I finished a complex program, especially when I built my first simple game on the computer with my own code. Since then, I decided to be a programmer and work in the tech industry because not only is this a growing field with an increasing demand for professionals but also CS is a subject I feel passionate about. Every accomplishment drives me to pursue more learning opportunities. I decided to be a CS major in college to learn more about CS and the tech industry. I hope to graduate as a prepared young professional.
